MIST & MATCH

Hello OptiMIST People!

The 22nd Marketing Insight Seminar and Training (MIST) presents an exciting pre-event that blends marketing with sport: MIST & MATCH.

The event features an engaging talk show session followed by a fun padel tournament, creating an atmosphere where participants can interact, connect, and compete in a casual yet interactive setting.

  • Venue: House of Padel, Agora Thamrin
  • Date: Sunday, 24 August 2025
  • Time: 08:00 - 16.00 WIB
  • Format: Doubles Mix
  • Level: Bronze
  • Fee: 500k / pair

Register now through the COURTSIDE app! (available on the App Store and Google Play Store). Let’s have fun together and don’t forget to stay #optiMIST!
