MARKETING
FESTIVAL

Monday – Tuesday, 2–3 February 2026

The Marketing Festival is designed for marketing enthusiasts who are eager to learn from real-world experiences and industry insights. It will include a four session seminar that will be split into two consecutive days. Each session will focus on a different marketing topic. In every session, three to four speakers from a range of industries will share their experiences, challenges, and lessons learned in their marketing journeys. With its diverse lineup and discussions, our seminar offers a unique chance to explore how marketing works across different fields while connecting theory with real-world application in an engaging and relatable way.
